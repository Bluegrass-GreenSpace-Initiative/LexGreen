Delete Your Account and Data

This page explains how to request deletion of your LexGreen account and any associated personal data or user‑generated content (e.g., photos you uploaded).

What will be deleted

  • Account credentials (email address and password hash) for staff/admin accounts.
  • User‑generated content you own, such as photos you uploaded.

What may be retained

  • Operational server logs retained for up to 90 days for security/abuse prevention.
  • Records we must keep to comply with law or enforce our terms.

How to request deletion

Please email your request to thea.francis@uky.edu from the email address associated with your account. Include:

  • Your account email address.
  • Whether you want full account deletion, or removal of specific content (e.g., photo links).

We will verify ownership of the account and process requests within 30 days.
