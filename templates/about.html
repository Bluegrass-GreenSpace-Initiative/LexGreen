About LexGreen

Nature Community Exploration Wellbeing

Our Mission

LexGreen helps University of Kentucky students and staff discover and connect with campus green spaces. By making information about our campus environment more accessible, we aim to enhance appreciation for UK's natural spaces.

The Project

This project is part of the University of Kentucky's initiative to promote environmental awareness and appreciation of campus green spaces. We integrate official tree inventory data with custom-designed interactive maps to create an engaging exploration experience.

Our Data

We use official tree inventory data from the University of Kentucky's Urban Forest Initiative, combined with carefully curated information about campus green spaces.
